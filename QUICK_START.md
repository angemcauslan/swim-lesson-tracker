## Common Questions

**Q: Will I lose my data?**  
A: No, unless you clear your browser data. Your information stays on your device.

**Q: Can I use this on multiple devices?**  
A: Yes! Install on each device. Currently, data doesn't sync between devices automatically.

**Q: Do I need internet?**  
A: Only for the first load and for AI report generation. Everything else works offline!

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: Yes! All data stays on YOUR device only. Nothing is sent to servers (except when using AI reports).

**Q: What if I accidentally delete the app?**  
A: Reinstall it the same way. Your data will still be there (unless you cleared browser data).

**Q: It's not working!**  
A: Check the Installation Guide (INSTALLATION_GUIDE.md) for detailed troubleshooting.
